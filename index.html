How the standing service runs.

Not a list of features. The actual mechanics of why it stays reliable when most office cleaning quietly slips after month one.

One documented standard
Every visit follows the same checklist, so the result doesn't depend on who happened to show up that night. The standard is written down, not carried in someone's head.
One person accountable
You deal with the owner, not a call center. If something's off, you say so once and I'm the one who fixes it. No layers, no shrugging.
Checked, not assumed
Irregular spot-checks against the standard, so quality doesn't drift between the times you happen to notice. The drift is the failure mode, so we watch for it.
The schedule holds
Sick days and turnover are my problem to absorb, not yours. The standing visit happens regardless of who's out, which is the entire point of a standing service.
